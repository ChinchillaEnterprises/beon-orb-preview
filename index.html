Replacing the existing Tuck logo with a new Beon orb per marketing direction. Each card below shows the orb in its two states side by side: the default state (black orb with yellow letter, used at idle) and the active state (yellow orb with black letter, used during interaction or when actively listening). Animations are now live CSS — speeds in v2 are roughly 2.5x slower than v1 to feel calm rather than urgent. The "b" letterform is approximated with Oswald 500 — we will swap in the official SVG when marketing provides it.

Layered usage recommendation

Idle (always-on): Option 2 (Breathe) at the v2 speed. Peter preferred Breathe over Pulse for idle in v1 feedback.

Hover / engaging: Option 4 (State Morph) but triggered by cursor, not on a loop.

Active processing: Option 3 (Rotating Ring) — reserved for "Beon is generating a response." Visible motion communicates work being done.

Avoid: stacking all four at once. Pulse plus breathe plus ring is visually busy and competes with surrounding content.
