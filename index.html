Animation movement options (reference)
Option 1
Halo Pulse
Yellow halo grows and fades · 4 second cycle (was 5s in v1.2)

Subtle, idle-friendly. The yellow halo grows and fades in a slow rhythm — draws the eye without distracting from content elsewhere on the page. Suggested use: always-on idle state.
Option 2
Breathe
Orb scales 1.0 to 1.12 · 5 second cycle
b
b
Calmer than the halo pulse. Whole orb expands and contracts. Gentler rhythm — reads as "alive but resting." Suggested use: alternative idle state if pulse feels too active.
Option 3
Rotating Ring
Ring rotates with two accent particles · 10 second rotation
b
b
Most attention-grabbing. Outer ring rotates with two glowing particles orbiting. Reads as "actively working." Suggested use: "Beon is processing" state — when an Ask Beon query is in flight.
Option 4
State Morph
Auto-cycles default ↔ active · 10 second loop
b
Demonstrates the transition between default and active states. In production this would only fire on hover or interaction, not as a continuous loop. Suggested use: hover transition between idle and engaged.

Layered usage recommendation

Topbar (black surface): yellow orb body with black "b" letter, halo pulse 4s — settled per Peter + Marta + Jamie.

Hero (light surface): black orb body with yellow letter, halo pulse 4s — primary recommendation. Yellow body on light is included for direct compare/reject only.

Active processing: Option 3 (Rotating Ring) — reserved for "Beon is generating a response."

Avoid: stacking all four animations at once. Pulse is the idle baseline; ring is the "working" signal; morph is reserved for interactive transitions.
